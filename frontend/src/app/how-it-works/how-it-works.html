How it works

From challenge to collective action — in seven steps

Start with a real-world problem. Move through fact-finding, collaborative drafting, expert review, and civic refinement — until a blueprint is ready for the world to adopt.

1

Challenges & Ideas

💡

Join policy challenges, share innovative ideas, and collaborate on solutions to pressing problems.

Inputs
A real-world problem, a civic concern, or a fresh idea for change
Platform checks
Community moderation, duplicate detection, scope validation
Outputs
A challenge or idea posted to the platform, open for collaboration
2

Fact-Finding

🔍

Identify the Truth. Before we solve, we must understand. The community gathers verified data, research, and lived experiences to define the true root of a problem.

Inputs
Community knowledge, research papers, lived experiences
Platform checks
Source verification, evidence quality ratings
Outputs
Verified data set and root cause analysis
3

Collaborative Drafting

📝

Build the Blueprint. Start with a guided template or import a draft. Cite evidence and refine the logic in a transparent, open-source space.

Inputs
Fact-finding results, guided templates, imported drafts
Platform checks
Evidence citations, duplicate detection, legality scope
Outputs
Shareable draft with inline citations and impact checklist
Typical duration
1–7 days
4

Wisdom Check

🧠

Verify the Path. Trusted Builders and Caretakers annotate drafts for feasibility, risks, and social impact, ensuring our solutions are grounded in reality.

Who
Rotating pool of domain experts with public disclosures; randomized assignment
Checks
Evidence validation, conflict-of-interest flags, credential verification
Outputs
Feasibility report, risk register, social impact assessment, budget estimate
Typical duration
3–10 days
5

Civic Refinement

🗳️

Unite through Logic. Our reputation system and civility tools help the most effective ideas rise, filtering out the noise of old political friction.

Signals
Reputation weighting, rate-limits for noise, down-ranking of uncivil posts
Outputs
Consolidated draft + set of vetted alternatives
Typical duration
5–14 days
6

Formulate Action

📜

Finalize the Plan. Once consensus is reached, proposals crystallize into actionable blueprints — high-quality solutions ready for our civilization to adopt.

Gate
Support + review thresholds achieved
Outputs
Actionable blueprint, voting brief, implementation tracker
Aftercare
Post-adoption audit and metrics reporting
7

Collective Resonance

📣

Drive the Change. We move beyond the platform. Using the weight of our community, users leverage social media and direct outreach to deliver blueprints to leaders, ensuring they are implemented.

Inputs
Finalized blueprint, community weight and momentum
Outputs
Blueprint delivered to decision-makers, tracked for real-world implementation

Quick answers

Who can participate?

Any verified citizen. Organizations can co-sponsor with disclosure. Every verified voice has equal standing in the process.

How are experts chosen for the Wisdom Check?

A rotating pool within domain expertise; assignments are randomized and all disclosures are public to prevent conflicts of interest.

What happens after Collective Resonance?

The community tracks real-world adoption of blueprints. Post-adoption audits and metrics reporting keep leaders accountable.

What counts as support?

Weighted by verification and reputation to reward constructive participation — not volume.
